sponsorship


The WICC-2018 seeks sponsors for its upcoming conference to be held at Sri Ramakrishna Institute of Technology, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, INDIA. We have limited and unlimited patronage opportunities available and it is first-come, first-served, so please act quickly! When you sponsor the event, you will:

We anticipate to have 500 participants from various parts of the country and abroad. The participants are PG Studtents / UG Students / Researcher / Teachers / Industry Representatives across the globe. Also we have more than 2300 students in the campus.

The Sponsor’s logo will be displayed in :

o Conference Brochures (reaching more than 500 engineering institutions in India)
o Conference Website (Anticipated hit of 2000 persons)
o Hoardings in prominent location of the conference halls (4 Parallel sessions in different halls)
o Conference Kits (500 participants)
o Hoardings in the Lunch area
o Publicity through our media partners (English and Tamil)
• Increase worldwide awareness of your organizations products and services
• Gain access to computer information society decision-makers

The following levels of sponsorships are available:
• Platinum – Rs.1 Lakh (1 No.)
• Gold – Rs. 50,000 (3 Nos.)
• Silver – 25,000 (4 Nos.)
• Associate Partner – Rs. 10,000 (10 Nos.)
Platinum, Gold and Silver sponsors will receive a number of complementary attendee passes. Three Officials from the organization shall attend the three day conference through this complementary passes.
